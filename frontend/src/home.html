About Us

L-Guard is a smart IoT device designed to enhance road safety for motorcycle riders and passengers. Our mission is to reduce fatality rates and improve accident response times by leveraging cutting-edge technology. With features like emergency alerts, accident tracking, and rider verification, we aim to make roads safer for everyone.

How It Works

1. Wear the Helmet

Equip the L-Guard smart helmet for enhanced safety.

2. Ride Safely

The helmet monitors your ride and detects accidents.

3. Get Help Instantly

Emergency alerts are sent to authorities in case of an accident.
